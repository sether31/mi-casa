How we met?

Our love

Our love began unexpectedly at bagong palengke, where we were practicing our dance for physical education. We were already done practicing and just playing around in that area with our classmates when she suddenly talked to me. It started as a simple, casual conversation, like a normal conversation with my classmates, but not until she noticed an old man passing by who looked to be in pain and unwell. When, out of the blue and with genuine concern, she approached the old man, she asked him if he was okay and if he's with someone, because the old man looked really unwell as he was limping to walk. I was caught off guard by it, and I don't know how to react, as I felt my heart pounding so deep for her and suddenly saw her indifferent ways as her eyes flashes, and her smiles gave me a warmth vibe for an "unknown reason". It was then that I realized that true kindness still exists in the world, and that day was the day I fell in love with her.
